About

FX-Mechanics

A one-person studio building audio plugins rooted in physical modelling, room acoustics and signal processing research, alongside original electronic and noise music.

This practice also feeds, and is fed by, academic research: I'm a lecturer and researcher in acoustics at ENSTA Paris, where structural vibration, wave propagation and signal processing meet sound design and composition. The tools developed here are born at that intersection: built for the lab and the listening room, and just as at home in a mixing or mastering studio, or a home studio.
